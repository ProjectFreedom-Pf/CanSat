About us

Project Freedom is a group of ambitious and dedicated year 12 students. We are a team representing Mill Hill County High School in the CanSat competition, run by the European Space Agency, which provides a unique and exceptional opportunity for students to apply their engineering and scientific knowledge in a practical setting. Inspired by our motto,

Inspired by our motto, "Open up the skye," we are committed to pushing boundaries, exploring new horizons, and making our vision take flight.

If you wish to see live updates or more about our project, please follow our social media.

Mission

Our primary mission involves designing and coding a mini satellite inside a can and launching it into the sky to collect and transmit data (such as temperature and pressure) from the atmosphere, and transmit it to the ground station.

Our Secondary mission involves successfully creating a landing gear.
